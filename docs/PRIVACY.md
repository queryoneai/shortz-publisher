# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-09

This Privacy Policy explains how ShortzForge collects, uses, and discloses personal information.

## Information We Collect
- Account information (email, display name)
- Content you upload (audio, text, video)
- Analytics and usage data

## How We Use Information
- Generate and host short-form videos from your content
- Provide publishing and scheduling integrations
- Improve and secure the service

## Sharing and Third Parties
We may share data with third-party services you connect (e.g., Buffer, TikTok, YouTube) to perform publishing on your behalf. Those services have their own policies.

## Data Retention
We retain user data as necessary to provide the service and in accordance with applicable law.

## Security
We use reasonable measures to protect data, but no service is completely secure.

## Your Choices
You can delete your account or disconnect third-party integrations at any time.
